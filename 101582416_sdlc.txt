Importance of DevOps in the Software Development Life Cycle

DevOps plays an important role in the software development life cycle.
It helps development and operations teams work together efficiently.

By learning DevOps, engineers can automate testing, deployment, and monitoring.
This reduces errors and speeds up software delivery.

DevOps practices improve software quality, system reliability, and customer satisfaction.
Learning DevOps makes the SDLC faster, more stable, and more collaborative.
