George Brown College

George Brown College is a well-known college in Toronto,
 Ontario, offering practical and technical education.
 The Casa Loma Campus, located at 160 Kendal Avenue, 
 focuses on hands-on programs like construction,
 engineering, and applied sciences. It has modern 
 classrooms, labs, study spaces, and is easily 
 accessible by public transit.

The campus also includes the Casa Loma Child Care Centre,
which provides childcare and a learning environment for
 Early Childhood Education students. Events like Campus
 Days and open houses allow students to explore programs
and services. Casa Loma Campus provides a supportive 
environment for students to gain skills and prepare for careers.
