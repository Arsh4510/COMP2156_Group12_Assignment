George Brown College is a prominent public college of applied arts and technology located in downtown
 Toronto, Ontario. Established in 1967, it is known for its focus on hands-on, real-world learning 
and its close connections to industry. The college offers a wide range of programs, including the
 Computer System Technician program, which prepares students for careers in IT infrastructure and 
networking. With campuses like St. James, Casa Loma, and the Waterfront, GBC provides a vibrant 
environment where students gain practical skills to succeed in the modern workforce.
